Our Expertise

In Worcester Fluids Technology we apply the experience and know-how gained throughout several years of engineering, research, development, installation and commissioning work in several industrial plants around the world, primarily in the steel industry.

Our Experience

Worcester Fluids Technology is a worldwide leader in Fluids Mechanics, Thermodynamics, Heat Transfer, Mechanical equipment and Fluids Systems design, supply installation, commissioning and operation. Our engineering team accumulates more than 80 years of experience around the world.

Our History

Worcester Fluids Technology started in 2011 as consultancy resource for the steel industry, focusing in Fluids Systems. Through the years has become a engineering, consultancy and systems’ supplier.

To this day Worcester Fluids Technology has representations in 6 countries around the world.

Our Team

Our select group of engineers and experts has worked for several years in high speed rolling mills around the world. Many of these installations currently hold world records of production, operating speeds, highest yields and mill utilization.

Each and every one of our engineers has many years of experience in engineering design, installation and commissioning work as well in trouble shooting.

The Founder

The company was founded in 2011, in Worcester, Massachusetts, U.S.A., by Senior Fluids Engineer Rafael Lazo, known in the industry for the development of the Smart Tank technology, developments in vacuum dehydration processes, developments in high speed cooling processes and related software and technics. Mr. Lazo is currently the President and CEO of the company.

The Company

Since then, the company has grown into a well-known development center for fluids system and technologies that include the Standard Worcester Oil Behavior test (SWOBT), the new generation of high efficiency filter elements W5 for lubrication systems and the ultr a-high-efficiency stripper nozzles for cooling and waxing systems.

From its headquarters in Massachusetts provides engineering and consultancy services, systems and products around the world.

Worcester Fluids Technology has also developed the most complete design software for industrial fluids systems providing design parameters, sizing and selection of components and assemblies, system pricing, overall weights, performance evaluation and automated completion of fluids circuit diagrams (back and forth).
